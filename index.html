When we first started working with Bessemer in 2006, the company had $45 billion in assets under management. Today, that number is over $110 billion, making Bessemer Trust one of the most successful trust companies. While advertising was by no means the sole lever contributing to this unparalleled achievement, it certainly helped drive thebrand in a single direction with a highly focused and powerful voice over an extended period of time. And that was something that few other brands have accomplished in this, or any other category.
